Game Animations: Werewolfby ~Pandadrake

Flash / Animations2010-2013 ~Pandadrake
Trying to make rough animations for my newbie flash game.
Here's the idle pose, attacking, and running.

I like the look of hand-drawn frame-by-frame animation, but it's so much more work than just tweening everything.

I am not that great at animating so critiques and comments are very welcome.


Sorry for the obnoxious size.

*edit 1-26-11
As suggested, tried making the running animation buckle around a bit to be less static.
